About Me

I'm a graduate researcher and cloud engineer based in Montreal. Currently pursuing an MSc in Information Security at Concordia University, where I work at the NSERC/Ericsson Industrial Research Chair in SDN/NFV Security, developing and evaluating novel security solutions for software-defined networking and network function virtualization.

Before joining Concordia, I worked as a DevOps Engineer at Pyxis, designing and maintaining cloud-based infrastructures on AWS and Linux. I also completed a Mitacs Research Internship at Ericsson working on industry-driven security challenges for telecom and cloud-native networks.

Earlier in my career I was a Research Assistant at the Apolo Scientific Computing Center at Universidad EAFIT, contributing to energy market research and HPC workloads. I hold a Bachelor's in Computer Science from EAFIT, where I received the Premio Desarrollo de Software.
