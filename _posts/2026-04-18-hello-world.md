## Why a blog?

I spend a lot of time reading write-ups, papers, and documentation.

This blog will cover three main areas:

- **Cloud & DevOps** — AWS patterns, Terraform, Kubernetes internals, things I run into at work.
- **Security research** — CTF write-ups, SDN/NFV security findings, and anything from my MSc work that I can share.
- **Tools & experiments** — small scripts, automation ideas, stuff worth documenting.

## What this is not

A polished publication. Expect half-finished ideas.
